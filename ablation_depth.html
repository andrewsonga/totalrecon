While removing depth supervision doesn't significantly hamper the rendered RGB, it induces several failure modes as shown in the 3D reconstructions: 1) Floating objects in the Human & Dog, Dog 1, Cat 2, Human 1 sequences 2) Objects that sink into the background in the Human & Cat sequence 3) Lower reconstructon quality.
